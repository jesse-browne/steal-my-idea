About

Have you ever come up with an amazing idea and just wanted to share it with someone who might make it happen?

Steal my idea is a free open source website you can use to post ideas you have come up with. It's for ideas you want to give away.

We believe that there are millions of ideas in the world which are seeking founders who can bring them to life. Little can stop a motivated entrepreneur with a good idea that's come at the right time. If you're an entrepreneur looking for an idea, look here! All the ideas are free and open intellectual property. The idea creators who posted the idea want to you steal it!

Who's behind this site? We are the co-founder team of www.greenpag.es, Katie Patrick and Jesse Browne. We started this site as a fun project to test drive django and as a place to put all our own ideas. We have lots of ideas we wanted other people to steal! Our friends had ideas as well, and so the site was born.

We hope other people will add their ideas, founders will discover them hence many beautiful unions to be made and the world to be a more innovative, fun and excellent place to live.

If you're a developer and like the sound of this you can help shape Steal My Idea. Feel free to fork the project on github and send us pull request. We'll acknowledge all contributors.
